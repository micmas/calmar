# Typicality-Based Training Order

## RTSS specification

**Target**: Semantic network organisation; efficiency of lexical access for low-typicality items.

**Ingredient**: Training items within a semantic category are sequenced from the most typical exemplar (highest typicality rating) to the least typical, so that the prototype is trained first before peripheral category members.

**Theory**: Prototype effects: typical exemplars have denser, more highly weighted connections in the semantic neighbourhood. Training the prototype first reactivates and stabilises the core semantic attractor for the category, providing a stronger scaffold for subsequent training of atypical members whose representations are more diffuse and susceptible to degradation.

## Used in therapies

- typicality_semantic_feature_analysis


## Addresses impairments

- anomic_aphasia
- anomia
- semantics


## Notes

The typicality manipulation is the distinguishing feature of this SFA variant relative to standard SFA. Evidence base is from Barbieri et al. 2023 (n=16 naming-treatment subgroup, RCT, formally extracted).
