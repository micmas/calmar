# Verbal Constraint

## RTSS specification

**Target**: Suppression of compensatory non-verbal strategies; forced engagement of damaged speech-language network.

**Ingredient**: Prohibition of gesture, drawing, writing, and pointing during treatment sessions; the patient is constrained to verbal communication as the sole output channel in structured communicative games.

**Theory**: Forced-use principle (from constraint-induced movement therapy): eliminating the 'easier' non-verbal route forces repeated engagement of damaged speech-language networks, preventing learned non-use and driving Hebbian strengthening of residual verbal pathways.

## Used in therapies

- constraint_induced_aphasia_therapy


## Addresses impairments

- brocas_aphasia
- anomic_aphasia
- fluency
