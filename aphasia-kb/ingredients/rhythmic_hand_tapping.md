# Rhythmic Left-Hand Tapping

## RTSS specification

**Target**: Articulatory timing; speech initiation.

**Ingredient**: Synchronised left-hand tapping on a surface at each syllable throughout melodic intoning, maintained across all four stages of MIT.

**Theory**: Left-hand tapping engages right-hemisphere somatomotor cortex (contralateral to left hand), creating an additional right-hemisphere locus of activation that temporally anchors articulatory onset. Rhythmic cueing is hypothesised to reduce initiation failures common in non-fluent aphasia by providing an external temporal scaffold for motor speech programming.

## Used in therapies

- melodic_intonation_therapy


## Addresses impairments

- brocas_aphasia
- apraxia_of_speech
- fluency
