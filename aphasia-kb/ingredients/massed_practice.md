# Massed Practice

## RTSS specification

**Target**: Activity-dependent synaptic plasticity at surviving perilesional language-network nodes.

**Ingredient**: Delivery of a high volume of practice trials (typically >100 per session) concentrated within sessions and across consecutive days, with minimal inter-session gaps.

**Theory**: Hebbian learning: repeated co-activation of pre- and post-synaptic elements strengthens synaptic efficacy via LTP-like mechanisms in surviving perilesional cortex. Meta-analytic dose–response data in aphasia (Bhogal et al. 2003) supports massed schedules producing larger effect sizes than distributed practice of equivalent total hours.

## Used in therapies

- constraint_induced_aphasia_therapy
- melodic_intonation_therapy
- spell_study_spell


## Addresses impairments

- brocas_aphasia
- anomic_aphasia
- fluency
- dysgraphia


## Notes

The dose parameter itself does not specify a target impairment; it amplifies the effect of whichever procedure it is combined with. Optimal session intensity and inter-session spacing remain active research questions.
